Our Story

A decade of nurturing beauty, now sharing it with you

Green Haven Nursery began as a dream shared by Margaret and Harold Thompson, a senior couple who discovered their passion for plants in their retirement. What started as a small backyard hobby blossomed into a thriving local nursery that has served our community for over ten years.

Every plant in our collection tells a story. Margaret personally selects each variety for its beauty, resilience, and ability to bring joy to homes. Harold ensures every plant receives the perfect balance of care, sunlight, and attention it needs to thrive. Together, they've built a reputation for quality, knowledge, and genuine care that extends beyond the sale.

Now, as we embrace the digital age, we're bringing our nursery to your doorstep. This website represents a new chapter in our journey, allowing us to share our carefully nurtured plants with plant lovers beyond our local community. While the platform is new, our commitment remains unchanged: to provide healthy, beautiful plants and the expertise to help them flourish in your care.

"We believe every home deserves the peace and beauty that plants bring. Our greatest joy is knowing that our plants become part of your family's daily life, bringing nature's calm into your space."

— Margaret & Harold Thompson
