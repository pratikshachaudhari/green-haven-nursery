Our Story

A small nursery built with patience, care, and a love for flowering plants.

From local nursery to online store

Green Haven began as a family-run nursery serving plant lovers offline for over 10 years. We carefully nurtured every flowering plant with the belief that a home feels warmer, calmer, and more alive with nature in it.

Today, we are bringing that same personal care online so customers can discover healthy, beautiful plants and have them delivered to their doorstep.

We focus on quality, thoughtful packaging, and friendly service so every order feels personal.
